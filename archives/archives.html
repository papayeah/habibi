    November 16th, 2023

    Compiling this museum the last few days, and looking back at it day after day, reinstated my pride as a designer. I'm proud of this work. More than my commercial work. This electric feeling, making things with my hands—insisting to code this website from scratch, spending hours catching up to current standards and practices, and working deep into the night—reminds me of a passage in George Lois' book Damn Good Advice. Advice #7.

    "If you don't burn out at the end of each day, you're a bum! ... I love the feeling of utter depletion: It is an ecstatic sense of having committed myself to the absolute limit. But after recharging at night, I'm ready to go the next morning. Isn't that what life is all about?"
